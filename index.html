What is CometHacks?

CometHacks is a 12-hour community event featuring presentations and workshops by both UTDallas campus organizations and our corporate sponsors. CometHacks is a day to learn something new, network with the STEM community, and build something awesome!

Any college student can participate! Registration is required.

Corporate Sponsors

Thank you to our corporate sponsors for providing food, prizes and workshops for this event! Some sponsors will present a workshop or presentation about what they do.

Why sponsor CometHacks?

Each of our sponsors will have the opportunity to send people from their organizations to join the event and optionally host a 1-3 hour hands-on workshop, technical talk, or informal presentation.

Additionally, your company can:

  • Promote your organization as an exciting, STEM-oriented place to have a career
  • Recruit well-rounded students
  • Get involved and network with leadership with STEM clubs at UTD
  • Show off products and get students to start using your platform
  • Access student resumes submitted through our online registration system
  • Host a talk or workshop on your company and its products
